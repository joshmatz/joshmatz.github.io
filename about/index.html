About

June 20, 2015

Hello there! I’m Josh Matz and I’m currently living in Austin, Texas with my wife, Rachael Matz, and dog Kindle. (There’s also a cat, Tonks, but I let Rachael take ownership of him.)

I’m a self taught web developer and received a BFA in Graphic Design at Baylor University. I’m currently working at InVision where I help make the world’s leading prototyping, collaboration & workflow platform. Previous employers include SourceClear (a security startup) and Springbox (a digital agency).

If you like my work, feel free to get in touch or catch me on Twitter @joshmatz.
