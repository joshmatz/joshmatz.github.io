About Me

Hello there! I’m Josh Matz and I’m currently living in Austin, Texas with my wife (just married!), Rachael Carter, and dog Kindle. (There’s also a cat, Tonks, but I let Rachael take ownership of him.)

I’m a self taught web developer and received a BFA in Graphic Design at Baylor University. I’m currently working at SourceClear, a small start up where I’m able to put my versatility to work on everything from email template design to full-on front-end development. Previously I worked at Springbox as a UX Developer, which was just my excuse to collaborate with nearly everyone on a project.

If you like my work, feel free to get in touch or catch me on Twitter @joshmatz.
